About

Overview:

The app’s purpose is to define or translate English-language terminology used in computer programming courses, for use by all students, whether or not English is a student’s first language.

Many students are learning English and computer programming at the same time. Technical terminology can often be unclear, complicated, or not explained well. The intent of this app is to function like a dictionary, or Wikipedia, as well as provide other resources (including course notes and learning resources).

General functionality includes:

  • View and browse a list of all terms.
  • Search for a term, in any language.
  • View details about a term, and its meaning in English and in other languages.
  • Add a new term, English, or in another language (including its first or initial definition).
  • Edit an existing term (English or other), and add another definition or data.
